Measurements
← Back to overview

This sheet allows you to record your body measurements in inches. Aim to measure each area at the same time of day for consistency.

Waist
in
Measure at the narrowest part of your torso, typically just above the belly button.
Chest
in
Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape horizontal.
Abdomen
in
Measure at the level of your belly button, ensuring the tape is snug but not tight.
Biceps
in
Flex your bicep and measure at its largest circumference.
Quadriceps
in
Measure around the thickest part of your thigh while standing.
