Day 4: Upper Push/Pull
← Back to overview

Duration: 60 minutes

Target muscle groups: Upper body (chest, shoulders, triceps, back, biceps)

Exercise level: Moderate intensity

Warm‑Up
— Slow — —
Get blood flowing to the joints and muscles.
Chest Press
3×12 Slow 45 s Machine
Stick chest up and start with shoulder blades protracted and arms out straight. Then, only go back as far as you can that feels comfortable.
↑ Superset ↓
Tricep Extensions
3×14 Slow 30 s Cables/EZ bar
Make sure you are squeezing your shoulder blades back to pull more with triceps and to lock the shoulder into place.
Incline Bench Press
3×10 Slow 45 s Machine/DBs
Go as low as you need to go to feel the lengthening of the muscle. If you start feeling anything in the shoulder, do not go lower than that.
Lateral Raise
3×10 Slow 45 s Machine
Raise the weights in a controlled manner, and only go up to about where your nose is. Set the seat to where your shoulders are in line with the weight.
Lat Pulldowns
3×12 Slow 30 s Machine/Cable
Ensure your shoulder blades are moving up and down but do NOT shrug. Lean back a little for extra lat activation.
↑ Superset ↓
Curls
3×10 Normal 30 s DBs
Slowed and controlled; make sure your chest is up and tall.
Rows
3×12 Slow 30 s Machine/Cable
Shoulder blades retracting and protracting. Pretend you are squeezing a pencil between your shoulder blades.
↑ Superset ↓
Hammer Curls
3×12 2‑1‑2 30 s DB
Neutral grip.
Tricep Extensions
3×12 Slow 30 s Cables/Rope
Make sure you are squeezing your shoulder blades back to pull more with triceps and to lock the shoulder into place.
↑ Superset ↓
Cable Curls
3×10 3‑1‑3 30 s Rope
Go through full ROM; you can stand in a staggered stance for extra balance.
